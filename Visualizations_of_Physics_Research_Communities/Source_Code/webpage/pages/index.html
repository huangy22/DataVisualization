Welcome to the world of Physics!

This website provides a high-level overview of communities in physics research. The analysis is based on articles published during the past decade on American Physical Society journals, such as Physical Review Letter, Physical Review X and Review of Modern Physics. All articles are grouped into different clusters based on their citation relations.

With the help of interactive visualizations, we want to help you to understand the high-level community structure in physics research, how different fields evolve in time, and identity the top physicists in different communities. We hope this website can provide you some insights about the research trend in physics.
